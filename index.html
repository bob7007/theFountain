The Fountain

Past, present, future; A man unwilling to accept the mortality of his sick wife, travels through time to find a cure. Back in the 16th century as a Spanish conquistador. Tomas takes on the task to find the fountain of youth for his queen. Continuing his mission, Tommy; a present-day scientist, experiments with different ideas in the lab trying to cure death. Looking up ahead at the future Tom hasn’t changed, but personal struggles and the difficulty nature of his task, finally force him to fulfil his wife’s request and let go of the past.
