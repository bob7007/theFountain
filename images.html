Visual effects

The fountain is a stunning looking film that takes place deep in the jungles of the Mayan territory, travelling through space and in some other remote locations. The picture takes a creative approach to its amazing cinematographic displays by utilizing macro photographic techniques and an understanding of fluid dynamics instead of solely computer-generated images. By taking advantage of the forces that naturally occur in water, such as: refraction, gravity and settlement the film is able to simulate the effects of deep space without CGI.
